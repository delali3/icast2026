Paper Submission

Requirements and process for submitting your research

Author Guidelines

Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference or periodicals simultaneously.

IEEE reserves the right to exclude papers from distribution after the conference if not presented at the event.

Plagiarism Policy Double Submission Policy

Paper Format

All submissions must be written in English and adhere to IEEE Conference Proceedings format.

Full Papers: 5-8 pages (including figures)
Short Papers: 3-4 pages
Accepted Formats: PDF, Word/ODT documents
Download Templates

Publication Requirements

To be published in ICAST 2026 Conference Proceedings and IEEE Xplore, authors must:

  • Register for the conference
  • Present the paper at the event
  • Pay non-refundable registration fees
  • Upload final IEEE-formatted paper

Note: One registration is valid for up to 3 papers.

Frequently Asked Questions about ICAST 2026

How do I submit my paper?

All paper submissions must be made through the Microsoft CMT platform. Create an account, follow the submission guidelines, and upload your paper in IEEE format.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. Detailed payment instructions will be provided upon registration.

What documents should I bring to the conference?

Please bring your registration confirmation, valid ID, and any presentation materials. International attendees should also carry their visa documents and passport.

Who can join at the live event venue?

The conference is open to registered participants, including researchers, academics, students, and industry professionals. All attendees must complete the registration process.

Will papers be published in IEEE Xplore?

Yes, all accepted and presented papers will be published in IEEE Xplore and indexed in Scopus, ensuring maximum visibility for your research.

Is virtual attendance available?

Yes, we offer virtual attendance options for those who cannot attend in person. Virtual participants can present their papers remotely and access all sessions.

What is the registration fee?

Registration fees vary based on category (IEEE member, non-member, student) and timing (early bird vs. regular). Please visit the pricing section for detailed information.

Are there accommodation options nearby?

Yes, there are several hotels near Academic City University including Holiday In Hotel, Labadi Beach Inn, and Cloud9 Luxury Apartments. Special rates may be available for conference attendees.
